# Data Collection Notice (DPDP §5) — Kinematic App

> **DRAFT for legal review.** This short notice must be shown/linked at every
> point where personal data is collected (web lead forms, the mobile apps'
> sign-in and attendance screens). It implements the itemised-notice duty in
> §5 of India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and doubles as a
> GDPR Art. 13 notice. Keep it plain-language and available in English and
> Hindi.

**Kaiyo Technology Labs** (the Data Fiduciary) is collecting your personal data.

**What we collect and why**
- Your **name, phone number, email, and the details you provide** — to create
  and manage your record in our customer's CRM and to contact you.
- If you are field staff: your **attendance selfie and precise location during
  working hours** — for workforce management and attendance verification.

**How it's used, shared and stored**
- Used only for the purposes above. Where your record sits in a customer's CRM,
  it is processed on behalf of **our business customer, who is the Data
  Fiduciary and instructs us** (Kaiyo Technology Labs acts as their processor).
- Shared with service providers who help run the app (hosting, notifications,
  and AI features). The list is at https://kinematicapp.com/privacy.
- Stored securely and **hosted in Australia — Supabase ap-southeast-2 (Sydney)**; some providers are outside
  India. Retained only as long as needed (see the full Privacy Policy).

**Your rights (DPDP §11–14)**
You may **access**, **correct**, or **erase** your data, **nominate** another
person, and **raise a grievance**. Contact the Grievance Officer:
**Sagar Bhargava, s@kinematicapp.com, 8802274880**. You may also complain to the **Data Protection
Board of India**.

**Consent**
By submitting this form / continuing, you consent to the processing described
above. You can **withdraw consent** at any time via emailing the Grievance Officer at s@kinematicapp.com — withdrawal
does not affect processing already carried out. Withdrawing may mean we can no
longer provide certain features.

Full details: **https://kinematicapp.com/privacy**.
